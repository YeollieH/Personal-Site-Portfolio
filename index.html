Who is Park Chanyeol?

Park Chan Yeol(박찬열),popularly known as Chanyeol, is a member of the South Korean-Chinese boy group "Exo"(from SM Entertainment), its sub-group Exo-K and sub-unit Exo-SC. He is a main rapper and sub vocalist.He won the 2nd place in the 2008 Smart Model Contest and became a trainee under SM Entertainment.After four years as a trainee,he debuted in 2012. In 2008 & 2010,he made cameo appearance in TVXQ's "Ha Ha Ha song" and Girl Generation's "Genie" and "Twinkle". He gained a lot of attention before his debut.He also appeared in a K Drama called "High Kick".He is within the top 10 of the most-followed K-pop idols on Instagram as of June 2020 and ranks No.5 with 22.7 million followers. Now,he has 23.3 million followers on Instagram. He is a Brand Ambassador of "Tommy Hilfiger" and "Parada" brands.And then, he is a muse of "Acqua Di Parama" brand. He has a "Studio NNG" which is created with SM Entertainment producer MQ. He enlisted into the military on March 29, 2021.

Award and Achievement

  • In 2015, Chanyeol won two awards,"Best New Actor" & "Hallyu Star Award" in the 'Korea Drama Awards' for his work in 'Exo Next Door'.
  • In 2016, he received "Most Popular Foreign Idol" from 'Top Chinese Music Award'.
  • In 2017, he accepted "Asia OST Popularity (with Punch)" from 'Asia Model Festival Awards' and nominated for "Best OST" award for his work in "Stay With Me" (with Punch). From 'YinYueTai VChart Awards', he received "Best Collaboration" for his work in "I Hate You" (with Yuan Shanshan) and Most Popular Artist (South Korea). In 'Melon Music Awards', he nominated for "Top 10 Artists".
  • In 2018, he received "Best Korean Singler of the Year" from 'KKBox Music Awards' for his work in "Stay With Me" (with Punch).
  • In 2019, he received "Most Popular Overseas Singer" from 'Tencent Music Entertainment Awards'.

2021 Present

Now, he is serving in the 9th Infantry Division or “White Horse Division” and is the leader of the 2nd platoon . He completed the basic training successfully and he is one of the top five people who received the Outstanding Manpower Award from 300 soldiers. He will play as a main role named "Raman" in “The Meisa's Song” which is military musical. He will be discharged in September 2022.
